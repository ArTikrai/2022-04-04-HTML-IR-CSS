About Us

IN 2012, A BRAVE TEAM BLESSED PORTLAND WITH A BEAUTIFUL GIFT: DELICIOUS MEATBALLS. BETWEEN TWO PIECES OF CRISP, BUTTERY BREAD OR OVER PASTA, AND SLATHERED IN SAUCE, THE BONTON TEAM KNEW IT WAS THE SAUCY COMFORT FOOD THE CITY NEEDED. OTHERS HAVE SINCE JOINED THE MEATBALL GAME, BUT NONE RISE TO THE GLORY OF THE ORIGINAL—THE MEATBALLS THAT RULE THEM ALL.

NOW, THEY’VE DOUBLED DOWN TO BRING PORTLAND A DREAM DUO: PIZZA AND MEATBALLS. DEEMED “NEIGHBORHOOD STYLE,” THIS CHEESY, TANGY COMBINATION IS THE SLICE TO RUIN THE REST. JOIN US ASAP AT BONTON TO GRAB A SLICE AND YOUR FAVORITE MEATBALL DISH.
